Frank Church Wilderness Research

The Frank Church area is so large it is covered by two huge double-sided maps at 1:100k. I have the north half map, which is mostly forested ridges. The only significant rugged, above-treeline area with lakes is the Bighorn Crags.

  • ++ scenic area
  • -- only one reasonable route in & out
  • -- it is the most heavily traveled area in the Frank Church
  • -- most of the lakes are within a day hike of the trailhead (ex: this report says airplane lake is 8-10 miles)
