Say you have a sum of N IID random variables, with each sample having mean 1. For various values of N and stdev, the probability that a run of N days will have negative value is:

|  sharpe | 5 days | 10 days | 20 days | 40 days | 60 days | 80 days  |
|---------+--------+---------+---------+---------+---------+----------|
|  0.1    | 0.412  | 0.376   | 0.327   | 0.264   | 0.219   | 0.186    |
|  0.15   | 0.369  | 0.318   | 0.251   | 0.171   | 0.123   | 0.090    |
|  0.2    | 0.327  | 0.264   | 0.186   | 0.103   | 0.061   | 0.037    |
|  0.25   | 0.288  | 0.215   | 0.132   | 0.057   | 0.026   | 0.013    |
|  0.3    | 0.251  | 0.171   | 0.090   | 0.029   | 0.010   | 0.004    |
|  0.4    | 0.186  | 0.103   | 0.037   | 0.006   | 0.001   | 0.000    |
|  0.5    | 0.132  | 0.057   | 0.013   | 0.001   | 0.000   | 0.000    |
|---------+--------+---------+---------+---------+---------+----------|
