Things I wish I knew

About:
  • Resumes -- many jobs of 2 years or less is a bad sign; it takes large companies about two years to fire a terrible performer
  • Interviews -- the person should be able to describe measureable accomplishments, not just actions. Having hobbies is a good litmus test for curiousity/energy.
  • Investment taxation -- the richest people do not pay taxes. The legal way to not pay taxes is to not realize gains. The more complex the investment, often the worse the taxes.
